On fields of green where dreams take flight,
Beneath the golden morning light,
A bat held firm, a ball set free,
Begins the game of destiny.

The bowler runs with burning fire,
The batter stands with calm desire.
A single stroke, a soaring six,
A crowd erupts with joyful kicks.

Each wicket tells a different tale,
Of courage found when hopes seem frail.
Each catch held tight, each run well-earned,
Rewards the lessons players learned.

It's more than trophies, more than fame,
More than winning every game.
For cricket teaches heart and grace,
Respect for every time and place.

Through sunny days and skies of gray,
The spirit never fades away.
With every cheer and every cheer's echo,
Friendship grows with every throw.

So let the willow sing once more,
Let leather race from shore to shore.
For cricket is not just a sport—
It's passion, pride, and life's true court.
